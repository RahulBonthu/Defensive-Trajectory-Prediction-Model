    Open the overlay image:

    ```bash
    open "/Users/rahulb/Desktop/Personal Projects/Defensive Trajectory Prediction Model/outputs/validation/50_play_overlay.png"
    ```

    Check for ALL of the following:

    1. **All offensive trajectories move left-to-right (positive X direction).** No play should show offensive players moving right-to-left.

    2. **The vertical red dashed line at x=0 represents the line of scrimmage.** Offensive players should start near or slightly behind x=0 and move forward (positive x).

    3. **No bimodal spread.** You should not see two mirror-image groups of trajectories (one going +x and one going -x). A bimodal spread means the direction flip was not applied.

    4. **Trajectories are on a reasonable scale.** The x-axis should span roughly -10 to +30 yards from the LOS. If all trajectories are shifted by ~10 yards (clustered around x=10 at snap), the absoluteYardlineNumber end-zone offset is missing.

    5. **The y-axis (lateral position) is centered near 0.** Players should spread symmetrically above and below 0 (field center).

    If you see issues:
    - Bimodal spread → direction flip bug in normalize_coordinates
    - Systematic +10 yard offset at snap → missing absoluteYardlineNumber + 10 in normalize_coordinates
    - Players facing "wrong way" in direction → angle flip applied after sin/cos encoding (wrong order)
  </how-to-verify>
